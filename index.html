Our Origin

Born from Memory & Magic

Every candle we pour carries an intention. Rooted in the earthy richness of the Pacific Northwest and the quiet mysticism of scent memory, Spark A Scents PDX was created to help you reconnect — to a place, a person, a feeling you didn't know you'd been missing.

We hand-select every note: foraged botanicals, aged woods, smoke-kissed resins. No shortcuts, no synthetics. Just pure, ritual-worthy fragrance.
